About Justo

Born in Peru and now living in Miami, FL, Justo has been cooking since childhood. He learned the art of baking from his mother, aunts, and the women in his life, and has spent years cooking for and raising his family.

Always sharing smiles through his meals and treats, he is now bringing one of his favorite sweets—the alfajor—to the public. You can find his creations at local farmers markets.
